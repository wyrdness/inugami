## Cultural Sensitivity Guidelines

> **Important**: When researching inugami folklore:
> 
> 1. **Recognize Real Harm**: Historical families faced actual discrimination
> 2. **Condemn Cruelty**: Animal cruelty is morally wrong and legally prohibited
> 3. **Academic Purpose**: Study for understanding, not practice
> 4. **Respect Victims**: Those stigmatized by beliefs deserve recognition
> 5. **Cultural Context**: Understand without endorsing harmful practices

## Research Ethics

### Responsible Scholarship

When studying dark folklore:
- Acknowledge harmful aspects honestly
- Recognize historical social impacts
- Maintain ethical boundaries
- Respect affected communities
- Present with appropriate context and warnings
